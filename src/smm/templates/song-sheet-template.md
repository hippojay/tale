## Dramatic Specifications

### Song Trigger
**Primary**: [from the Song Trigger Framework]
**Secondary** (if applicable): [from the Song Trigger Framework]

Song Trigger Framework:
1. Declaration of Want — character must voice deepest desire or dream
2. Emotional Overflow — feeling exceeds what speech can carry
3. Conflict Escalation — argument speech can no longer contain
4. Decision/Revelation — character at a turning point, processing internally
5. Communal Expression — group must speak/feel/act as one
6. Comic Exaggeration — situation so absurd only music can match it
7. Seduction/Persuasion — character winning over another person or the audience
8. Narrative Compression — story too long to tell in dialogue
9. Thematic Crystallisation — the show's central idea crystallised
10. Reprise/Echo — earlier moment revisited with new meaning
