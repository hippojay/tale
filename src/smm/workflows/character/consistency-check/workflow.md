# Character Consistency Check Workflow

## Objective
Verify character consistency across chapters and scenes.

## Prerequisites
- Written chapters to review
- Story Bible with character details

## Steps

### 1. Load References
- Open Story Bible character section
- Review character profiles
- Note established traits and voice

### 2. Physical Consistency
- Eye color, hair, height consistent?
- Clothing appropriate to timeline?
- Injuries/changes tracked correctly?
- Age references accurate?

### 3. Personality Consistency
- Actions align with established traits?
- Reactions match character psychology?
- Growth follows arc, not random?
- Flaws still present (unless resolved)?

### 4. Voice Consistency
- Speech patterns maintained?
- Vocabulary level consistent?
- Verbal tics present?
- Emotional expression style matches?

### 5. Knowledge Consistency
- Character only knows what they've learned?
- No anachronistic knowledge?
- Secrets maintained until revealed?
- Learning moments shown?

### 6. Relationship Consistency
- Dynamics match established patterns?
- Changes are earned and shown?
- History references accurate?
- Power dynamics maintained?

### 7. Timeline Consistency
- Character location makes sense?
- Travel time accounted for?
- Simultaneous events possible?
- Age progression accurate?

### 8. Document Issues
- List inconsistencies found
- Note chapter and line
- Suggest corrections
- Prioritize by severity

### 9. Update Story Bible
- Add any new established facts
- Flag contradictions for resolution
- Update character states

## Output
- Consistency report with issues
- Updated Story Bible
