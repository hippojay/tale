# Final Proof Workflow

## Objective
Final proofread pass before publication — catch any remaining errors.

## Steps

### 1. Clean Read
- Read manuscript fresh, as a reader would
- Mark anything that causes a stumble
- Note any remaining typos or errors

### 2. Mechanical Check
- Spelling errors missed by earlier passes
- Punctuation errors (especially quotation marks, em dashes)
- Missing or extra words
- Doubled words or phrases

### 3. Formatting Check
- Page/chapter breaks correct
- Headers and footers consistent
- Font consistency throughout
- Indentation and spacing uniform

### 4. Front and Back Matter
- Title page accurate
- Copyright information correct
- Dedication and acknowledgments proofed
- Table of contents matches chapter titles
- About the author section clean

### 5. Final Dialogue Check
- Opening and closing quotation marks paired
- Dialogue attribution clear
- No orphaned dialogue tags

### 6. Last Pass
- Read final pages carefully (fatigue errors common at end)
- Verify last line/paragraph is intentional and clean
- Confirm chapter numbering sequential

## Output
- Proofread manuscript (clean copy)
- Final corrections list
- Publication readiness confirmation
