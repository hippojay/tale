# Opening Pages Critique Workflow

## Objective
Intensive review of first chapter/pages for hook strength.

## Prerequisites
- Opening chapter(s) written
- Genre identified

## Steps

### 1. First Line Analysis
- Does it hook?
- Does it raise questions?
- Is it memorable?
- Does it fit genre/tone?

### 2. First Paragraph
- Is reader grounded?
- Is voice established?
- Is there forward momentum?
- Would reader continue?

### 3. First Page
- Is protagonist introduced?
- Is situation clear?
- Is there tension/conflict?
- Is world hinted?

### 4. First Chapter
- Is inciting incident present or imminent?
- Is character compelling?
- Are stakes suggested?
- Is genre clear?
- Would reader buy the book?

### 5. Opening Sins Check
Avoid:
- Weather opening
- Waking up opening
- Mirror description
- Info dump
- Prologue that delays story
- Unnamed protagonist
- Passive protagonist
- No conflict

### 6. Genre-Specific Requirements
**Thriller:** Immediate tension
**Romance:** Meet-cute or emotional hook
**Mystery:** Crime or intrigue
**Fantasy:** Wonder or world-hook
**Literary:** Voice or character hook

### 7. Promise Evaluation
- What does opening promise?
- Is promise compelling?
- Can story deliver?

### 8. Comparison Test
- Would this compete with published books?
- Would agent/editor read on?
- Would reader buy?

### 9. Recommendations
- Specific line edits
- Restructuring suggestions
- Elements to add
- Elements to cut
- Alternative openings to consider

## Output
- Opening pages critique report
- Specific revision recommendations
- Alternative opening suggestions
