# Character Presence Workflow

## Objective
Enhance character physical and emotional presence in scenes.

## Prerequisites
- Scene/chapter to enhance
- Character profiles from Story Bible

## Steps

### 1. Assess Current Presence
- Are characters physically grounded?
- Do they feel embodied?
- Is emotional state visible?

### 2. Physical Grounding
Add where missing:
- Body position/posture
- Movement and gesture
- Facial expressions
- Physical habits/tics

### 3. Emotional Visibility
Show emotion through:
- Physical manifestations (tight jaw, clenched fists)
- Breathing changes
- Voice quality shifts
- Involuntary responses

### 4. Character-Specific Details
From Story Bible:
- Unique mannerisms
- Habitual gestures
- Physical characteristics
- Clothing/appearance

### 5. Interaction with Environment
- How do they occupy space?
- What do they touch/notice?
- How does setting affect them?

### 6. Dialogue Enhancement
- Action beats between lines
- Physical reactions to others' words
- Subtext through body language

### 7. POV Character Depth
If POV character:
- Internal sensations
- Emotional interiority
- Physical awareness

### 8. Balance Check
- Not overdone?
- Serves scene purpose?
- Maintains pacing?

## Output
- Enhanced scene with stronger character presence
- Notes on techniques used
