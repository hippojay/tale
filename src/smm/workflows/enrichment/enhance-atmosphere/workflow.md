# Enhance Atmosphere Workflow

## Objective
Strengthen mood and atmospheric elements in scene/chapter.

## Prerequisites
- Content to enhance
- Understanding of intended mood

## Steps

### 1. Identify Target Mood
- What emotion should reader feel?
- Tension, dread, romance, wonder, melancholy?

### 2. Assess Current Atmosphere
- What mood exists now?
- What's working?
- What's undermining it?

### 3. Setting as Mood
- Weather reflecting emotion
- Lighting choices
- Space (cramped/open)
- Temperature
- Time of day

### 4. Sensory Mood Building
- Sounds that evoke mood
- Smells that trigger emotion
- Textures that reinforce feeling
- Colors that set tone

### 5. Pacing for Mood
- Sentence length (short=tension, long=languor)
- Paragraph breaks
- White space usage
- Scene rhythm

### 6. Word Choice
- Connotation over denotation
- Sound of words (harsh/soft)
- Imagery that reinforces mood

### 7. Character Response
- How does POV character feel the atmosphere?
- Physical responses to environment
- Emotional coloring of perception

### 8. Consistency Check
- Mood sustained throughout?
- No jarring breaks?
- Transitions smooth?

## Output
- Atmospherically enhanced content
- Notes on mood techniques used
