# Worldbuild Scene Workflow

## Objective
Deepen world immersion in specific scene.

## Prerequisites
- Scene to enrich
- Story Bible world details

## Steps

### 1. Assess Current World Presence
- How grounded is the scene?
- What world details exist?
- What's missing?

### 2. Setting Integration
- Specific location details
- Historical/cultural context
- Environmental factors
- Time of day/season effects

### 3. World Rules Presence
- Magic/technology visible?
- Social structures shown?
- Cultural norms demonstrated?
- Economic realities present?

### 4. Sensory World-Building
- What does this world sound like?
- What are its smells?
- What textures exist?
- What colors dominate?

### 5. Character-World Interaction
- How do characters interact with setting?
- What's familiar vs. notable to them?
- What do they take for granted?

### 6. Background Details
- Other people present?
- Activity in environment?
- Signs of the world's history?

### 7. Balance Check
- World-building serves story?
- Not info-dumping?
- Pacing maintained?

### 8. Consistency Verification
- Matches Story Bible?
- No contradictions?
- Update Bible if new details

## Output
- Enriched scene with world depth
- Story Bible updates
