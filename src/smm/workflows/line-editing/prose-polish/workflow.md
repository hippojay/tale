# Prose Polish Workflow

## Objective
Final prose polish for publication readiness.

## Steps

### 1. Eliminate Weak Constructions
- "There was/were" → stronger construction
- Passive voice → active (where appropriate)
- "Started to/began to" → direct action

### 2. Strengthen Verbs
- Replace "was + ing" with past tense
- Eliminate adverb + weak verb
- Choose precise verbs

### 3. Tighten Prose
- Cut unnecessary words
- Combine choppy sentences
- Break run-ons appropriately

### 4. Enhance Imagery
- Make abstract concrete
- Strengthen sensory details
- Sharpen metaphors

### 5. Flow Check
- Read aloud
- Smooth transitions
- Verify rhythm

## Output
- Polished prose
- Before/after examples
