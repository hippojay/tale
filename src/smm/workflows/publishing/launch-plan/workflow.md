# Launch Plan Workflow

## Objective
Create comprehensive book launch strategy for maximum impact.

## Steps

### 1. Launch Timeline
- Set launch date (or target window)
- Work backward: 6 months, 3 months, 1 month, 1 week milestones
- Identify key pre-launch activities
- Plan post-launch sustain period

### 2. Platform Assessment
- Author's existing platform (social media, email list, website)
- Gaps to fill before launch
- Platform building priorities
- Content calendar for pre-launch period

### 3. Pre-Launch Strategy
- Cover reveal timing and channels
- ARC (Advance Reader Copy) distribution plan
- Pre-order strategy (if applicable)
- Endorsement and blurb collection
- Media kit preparation

### 4. Launch Week Plan
- Day-by-day activity schedule
- Social media content prepared
- Email sequences drafted
- Launch events (virtual or in-person)
- Cross-promotion partnerships

### 5. Marketing Channels
- Social media strategy by platform
- Email marketing plan
- Book blogger and reviewer outreach
- Podcast and interview opportunities
- Paid advertising budget and targeting

### 6. Post-Launch Sustain
- First 30 days activity plan
- Review generation strategy
- Ongoing content plan
- Series planning (if applicable)
- Backlist strategy

### 7. Budget and Resources
- Marketing budget allocation
- Professional services needed (cover design, formatting, ads)
- Time commitment realistic assessment

## Output
- Complete launch plan with timeline
- Marketing channel strategy
- Budget breakdown
- Content calendar
