# Market Analysis Workflow

## Objective
Analyze market positioning and competition.

## Steps

### 1. Genre Placement
- Primary genre/category
- Sub-genre
- Age category

### 2. Comp Titles
- Find 3-5 comparable books
- Published in last 3-5 years
- Similar tone, audience, concept

### 3. Market Position
- What's similar to existing books?
- What's different/unique?
- Gap in market?

### 4. Audience
- Who is the reader?
- What do they want?
- Where do they find books?

### 5. Trends
- Current genre trends
- Rising or declining interest?
- Timing considerations

### 6. Competition
- Who are the big names?
- Recent successes?
- Oversaturated areas?

### 7. Positioning Statement
- "For readers who loved X, this book offers Y"
- Unique selling proposition

## Output
- Market analysis report
- Comp title list with rationale
- Positioning statement
