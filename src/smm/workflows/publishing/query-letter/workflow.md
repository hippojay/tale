# Query Letter Workflow

## Objective
Craft compelling query letter for agents.

## Steps

### 1. Hook
- One sentence that grabs attention
- Unique angle or concept
- Voice hint

### 2. Setup
- Protagonist introduction
- Initial situation
- What they want

### 3. Conflict
- What stands in the way
- Stakes
- Central tension

### 4. Choice/Stakes
- What must protagonist do?
- What happens if they fail?
- Why should we care?

### 5. Metadata
- Title
- Word count
- Genre/category
- Comp titles (2-3)

### 6. Bio
- Relevant credentials
- Publication history (if any)
- Why you wrote this

### 7. Polish
- Under 300 words (body)
- Voice matches book
- No rhetorical questions
- No clichés

### 8. Personalization
- Why this agent?
- Specific to their interests

## Output
- Query letter draft
- Personalization notes
