# Synopsis Workflow

## Objective
Write compelling synopsis for submission.

## Steps

### 1. Opening
- Protagonist and situation
- Inciting incident
- What's at stake

### 2. Act One
- Key events
- First major turning point
- Protagonist's commitment

### 3. Act Two
- Major complications
- Midpoint shift
- Rising stakes
- Dark moment

### 4. Act Three
- Climax
- Resolution
- Character transformation

### 5. Key Elements
- Include ALL major plot points
- Reveal the ending
- Show character arc
- Convey emotional journey

### 6. Format
- Present tense
- Third person
- 1-2 pages (single-spaced)
- Focus on main plot

### 7. Polish
- Clear cause and effect
- Emotional beats included
- Voice present but subtle
- No cliffhangers

## Output
- Synopsis (short: 1 page)
- Synopsis (long: 2-3 pages)
