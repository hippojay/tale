# Chapter Review Workflow

## Objective
Provide comprehensive, constructive review of chapter content with specific, actionable suggestions for improvement.

## Prerequisites
- Completed chapter draft
- Story Bible (story-bible.md) for continuity verification
- Story Requirements Document (SRD) for genre and tone expectations
- Previous chapters for continuity (if applicable)

## Workflow Steps

### 1. Initial Read
- Read chapter completely without stopping
- Note overall impression and reader experience
- Identify emotional high points and low points
- Assess whether chapter delivers on its promise

### 2. Structural Analysis
- Evaluate chapter opening hook strength
- Assess scene transitions and flow
- Check pacing - identify slow spots or rushed moments
- Verify chapter ending hook effectiveness
- Confirm chapter advances plot or character meaningfully

### 3. Character Evaluation
- Check character voice consistency
- Assess motivation clarity
- Verify character actions align with established personality
- Evaluate character growth or change in chapter
- Check dialogue authenticity and effectiveness

### 4. Plot and Logic
- Verify plot progression makes sense
- Identify any plot holes or logic gaps
- Check cause-and-effect relationships
- Assess conflict presence and escalation
- Confirm stakes are clear

### 5. Continuity Check
- Load story-bible.md and verify consistency
- Check character details against established facts
- Verify world details and rules
- Confirm timeline consistency
- Flag any contradictions with previous content

### 6. Craft Elements
- Assess descriptive balance and sensory engagement
- Evaluate dialogue-to-narrative ratio
- Check POV consistency and effectiveness
- Review sentence variety and rhythm
- Identify repetitive words or phrases

### 7. Reader Experience
- Evaluate emotional engagement
- Assess clarity - any confusing moments?
- Check for page-turning quality
- Identify moments that drag or lose interest
- Verify genre expectations are met

### 8. Strengths Identification
- Highlight what's working well
- Identify strongest scenes or moments
- Note effective character beats
- Recognize successful craft techniques
- Acknowledge voice and style strengths

### 9. Improvement Suggestions
- Prioritize issues by impact (major to minor)
- Provide specific, actionable recommendations
- Suggest concrete solutions, not just problems
- Offer alternatives for weak areas
- Include examples where helpful

### 10. Review Report
- Summarize overall assessment
- List strengths (what to preserve)
- Detail prioritized improvement areas
- Provide specific action items
- Include encouragement and next steps

## Output Format
- Review report: `{output_folder}/reviews/chapter-{number}-review.md`
- Structure: Overall Assessment, Strengths, Issues by Priority, Action Items
- Use constructive, supportive tone throughout

## Review Report Template

```markdown
# Chapter {number} Review

## Overall Assessment
[2-3 paragraphs on general impression and reader experience]

## Strengths
- [Specific strength with example]
- [Specific strength with example]
- [Continue...]

## Priority 1: Major Issues
[Issues that significantly impact story effectiveness]
- **Issue**: [Description]
  - **Impact**: [Why this matters]
  - **Suggestion**: [Specific fix]

## Priority 2: Moderate Issues
[Issues that affect quality but don't break the story]
- **Issue**: [Description]
  - **Suggestion**: [Specific fix]

## Priority 3: Minor Polish
[Small improvements for refinement]
- [Quick fix items]

## Action Items
1. [Specific action]
2. [Specific action]
3. [Continue...]

## Continuity Notes
[Any continuity questions or concerns]

## Encouragement
[Positive closing remarks and next steps]
```

## Notes for Agent
- Always start with strengths before addressing issues
- Be specific - vague feedback isn't actionable
- Offer solutions, not just criticism
- Maintain supportive, constructive tone
- Prioritize ruthlessly - focus on what matters most
- Consider author's experience level in explanation depth
