# Continuity Check Workflow

## Objective
Verify continuity against Story Bible and previous content.

## Prerequisites
- Content to check
- Story Bible
- Previous chapters

## Steps

### 1. Character Continuity
- Physical descriptions match?
- Personality consistent?
- Knowledge appropriate (no future knowledge)?
- Relationships accurate?
- Location possible given timeline?

### 2. World Continuity
- Setting details consistent?
- World rules followed?
- Technology/magic consistent?
- Cultural details accurate?

### 3. Timeline Continuity
- Events in correct order?
- Time passage logical?
- Day/night appropriate?
- Season consistent?
- Character ages correct?

### 4. Object Continuity
- Items where they should be?
- Objects introduced before used?
- Chekhov's guns tracked?

### 5. Plot Continuity
- Cause and effect logical?
- No contradictory events?
- Foreshadowing consistent?
- Reveals don't contradict setup?

### 6. Dialogue Continuity
- References to past events accurate?
- Character knowledge appropriate?
- Promises/threats tracked?

### 7. Document Issues
For each issue:
- Location (chapter/page)
- Nature of inconsistency
- What it contradicts
- Suggested fix

### 8. Update Story Bible
- Add new established facts
- Flag contradictions
- Update character states

## Output
- Continuity report
- List of issues with fixes
- Updated Story Bible
