# Continuity Check Workflow

## Objective
Systematically verify chapter consistency against Story Bible, previous chapters, and established narrative to prevent contradictions in characters, locations, timeline, backstory, and world rules.

## Prerequisites
- Completed chapter draft
- Story Bible (authoritative reference)
- Previous chapters (for cross-reference)
- Story Requirements Document (for thematic/tonal consistency)

## Steps

### 1. Load Reference Materials

**Load Story Bible sections:**
- Character profiles (physical descriptions, personality, backstory, relationships)
- World details (locations, rules, magic/technology systems, cultures)
- Timeline (chronology of events, character ages, seasonal progression)
- Object tracking (important items, Chekhov's guns)
- Established facts (anything stated as true in previous chapters)

**Load previous chapters:**
- Identify all chapters that precede this one chronologically
- Note any references to future events (foreshadowing)
- Track character states at end of previous chapter

**Load SRD:**
- Genre conventions to maintain
- Tone and style guidelines
- Thematic elements to preserve

### 2. Character Consistency Audit

**For each character appearing in this chapter:**

**Physical Descriptions:**
- Does appearance match Story Bible? (hair color, eye color, height, build, distinguishing features)
- Are any new physical details introduced? (document for Bible update)
- Do descriptions contradict previous chapters?
- Is character's age appropriate for timeline position?

**Personality & Behavior:**
- Does character act consistently with established personality?
- Are behavioral patterns maintained? (speech patterns, mannerisms, habits)
- Do reactions align with character's psychology and values?
- Are any out-of-character moments justified by circumstances?

**Knowledge & Memory:**
- Does character know only what they should know at this point?
- No anachronistic knowledge (knowing future events)?
- References to past events accurate?
- Character remembers established facts from previous chapters?

**Relationships:**
- Are character dynamics consistent with established relationships?
- Relationship progression logical given timeline?
- No contradictions in how characters address each other?
- Emotional states toward other characters appropriate?

**Character State:**
- Physical condition consistent? (injuries, illnesses, pregnancy, etc.)
- Emotional state follows from previous chapter?
- Skills/abilities match established capabilities?
- Possessions/resources available make sense?

**Location & Timeline:**
- Is character's presence in this location possible given timeline?
- Travel time from previous location realistic?
- Character can't be in two places at once?

### 3. Location & Setting Consistency

**For each location in this chapter:**

**Physical Details:**
- Does location description match Story Bible?
- Are architectural details consistent?
- Geography/layout matches previous descriptions?
- Weather/climate appropriate for season and region?

**Sensory Details:**
- Sounds, smells, textures consistent with location type?
- Lighting appropriate for time of day?
- Temperature/atmosphere matches season?

**World Rules:**
- Magic/technology systems function consistently?
- Cultural norms observed (or violations justified)?
- Social structures maintained?
- Economic realities consistent?

**Accessibility:**
- Can characters reach this location given their starting point?
- Travel time realistic?
- Required permissions/access obtained?

### 4. Timeline & Chronology Audit

**Event Sequencing:**
- Events occur in logical order?
- No temporal paradoxes?
- Flashbacks/flash-forwards clearly marked?

**Time Passage:**
- Time elapsed since previous chapter makes sense?
- Day/night cycle accurate?
- Meal times appropriate?
- Sleep/rest periods accounted for?

**Calendar Consistency:**
- Date/day of week correct?
- Season appropriate for calendar position?
- Holidays/festivals at correct times?
- Character ages match timeline?

**Cause & Effect:**
- Sufficient time for consequences to manifest?
- No effects before causes?
- Character recovery times realistic (injuries, exhaustion)?

### 5. Backstory & History Consistency

**Character Backstories:**
- References to character pasts match established backstory?
- No contradictions in origin stories?
- Family relationships consistent?
- Past events referenced accurately?

**World History:**
- Historical references accurate to world timeline?
- Past wars/events consistent with established history?
- Cultural evolution logical?
- Legends/myths consistent?

**Revealed Information:**
- New backstory revelations don't contradict previous hints?
- Mysteries maintain internal logic?
- Retcons avoided (or explicitly acknowledged)?

### 6. Object & Item Tracking

**Important Objects:**
- Items appear where they should be?
- Objects introduced before used?
- Magical/special items function consistently?
- Weapons/tools available when needed?

**Chekhov's Guns:**
- Significant items from earlier chapters tracked?
- Foreshadowed objects appear when promised?
- No forgotten plot-critical items?

**Possessions:**
- Characters have access to their belongings?
- Lost/stolen items not magically reappearing?
- Gifts/inheritances tracked?

### 7. Plot & Narrative Consistency

**Plot Logic:**
- Events follow logically from previous chapters?
- No plot holes introduced?
- Character decisions make sense given their knowledge?
- Motivations remain consistent (or evolution justified)?

**Foreshadowing:**
- Payoffs match earlier setup?
- Promises to reader kept?
- Red herrings don't contradict actual truth?

**Reveals & Twists:**
- New information doesn't contradict established facts?
- Surprises are fair (clues were present)?
- Retcons avoided?

**Subplots:**
- Secondary storylines progress logically?
- No forgotten subplot threads?
- Subplot events don't contradict main plot?

### 8. Dialogue & Communication Consistency

**Character Voice:**
- Speech patterns match established voice?
- Vocabulary appropriate for character education/background?
- Dialect/accent consistent?
- Verbal tics maintained?

**Information Exchange:**
- Characters reference past conversations accurately?
- Promises/threats from previous chapters remembered?
- Secrets maintained (or reveals justified)?
- Lies tracked (characters remember their own lies)?

**Knowledge Sharing:**
- Information spreads realistically?
- Characters don't know things they weren't told?
- Gossip/rumors tracked?

### 9. Thematic & Tonal Consistency

**Theme Tracking:**
- Thematic elements from SRD maintained?
- Symbolic motifs consistent?
- Character arcs align with themes?

**Tone:**
- Genre conventions maintained?
- Narrative voice consistent with previous chapters?
- Humor/darkness level appropriate?
- Pacing style matches established rhythm?

### 10. Document All Issues

**For each inconsistency found, document:**

**Issue Details:**
- **Location:** Chapter X, paragraph/line number
- **Type:** Character/Location/Timeline/Backstory/Object/Plot/Dialogue/Theme
- **Severity:** Critical (breaks story logic) | Major (noticeable contradiction) | Minor (small detail)
- **Description:** What is inconsistent
- **Contradiction:** What it contradicts (cite Story Bible or chapter reference)
- **Impact:** How this affects reader experience or story logic

**Suggested Fixes:**
- **Option 1:** [Fix in current chapter]
- **Option 2:** [Update Story Bible/previous chapter]
- **Option 3:** [Alternative approach]
- **Recommendation:** [Which fix is best and why]

### 12. Update Story Bible

**Add new established facts:**
- Character details introduced in this chapter
- Location details revealed
- Timeline events
- Object introductions
- Backstory revelations

**Flag contradictions:**
- Mark Bible entries that conflict with this chapter
- Note which version is canonical
- Document resolution decision

**Update character states:**
- Physical condition at chapter end
- Emotional state
- Location
- Possessions
- Knowledge gained

## Output

- **Continuity Check Report** — Comprehensive analysis with all issues documented
- **Revision Checklist** — Prioritized fixes
- **Story Bible Updates** — New facts and clarifications
- **Saved to:** `{output_folder}/continuity-checks/chapter-[X]-continuity.md`

## Usage Notes

**When to use this workflow:**
- After completing chapter draft (before review)
- Before moving to next chapter
- After major revisions that change established facts
- When you suspect a continuity error

**Critical rule:**
- If critical issues found, STOP and fix before proceeding
- Don't write next chapter until continuity is clean
- Continuity errors compound — fix early

**Integration with other workflows:**
- Run AFTER `/smad-smm-write-chapter` or `/smad-smm-revise-chapter`
- Run BEFORE `/smad-smm-chapter-review`
- Use results to update `/smad-smm-create-story-bible`

**For series/multi-book projects:**
- Check against previous books' Story Bibles
- Maintain master timeline across books
- Track character aging across series

**Automation potential:**
- Can be part of automated audit chain
- Trigger after chapter completion
- Block next chapter if critical issues found
