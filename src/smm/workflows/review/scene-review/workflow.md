# Scene Review Workflow

## Objective
Focused review of individual scene.

## Prerequisites
- Scene to review
- Story Bible
- Scene objectives/outline

## Steps

### 1. Scene Purpose Check
- What should scene accomplish?
- Does it accomplish it?
- Is it necessary?

### 2. Structure Review
- Clear beginning, middle, end?
- Conflict present?
- Change occurs?
- Hook at end?

### 3. Character Review
- POV consistent?
- Motivation clear?
- Reactions believable?
- Voice authentic?

### 4. Dialogue Review
- Purposeful?
- Distinct voices?
- Subtext present?
- Natural flow?

### 5. Pacing Review
- Appropriate length?
- Momentum maintained?
- Balance of elements?

### 6. Craft Review
- Description effective?
- Sensory engagement?
- Word choice strong?

### 7. Continuity Check
- Consistent with Story Bible?
- Timeline accurate?
- Details correct?

### 8. Strengths & Issues
- What works well?
- What needs improvement?
- Priority of fixes?

## Output
- Scene review report
- Specific improvement suggestions
