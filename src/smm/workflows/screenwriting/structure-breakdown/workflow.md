# Screenplay Structure Breakdown Workflow

## Objective
Analyze existing screenplay structure or create a structural blueprint for a new one.

## Steps

### 1. Determine Mode
- **Analysis mode**: Break down an existing screenplay's structure
- **Creation mode**: Build structure from concept/outline

### 2. Three-Act Mapping
Identify or design:
- **Act 1 (Setup)**: ~25% of total pages
  - Opening image / tone-setter
  - Protagonist introduction (visual, defining moment)
  - Establish world and status quo
  - Inciting incident (the event that disrupts everything)
  - Debate / refusal of the call
  - Act 1 break: protagonist commits to the journey

- **Act 2 (Confrontation)**: ~50% of total pages
  - New world / new rules
  - Rising complications and escalating stakes
  - Midpoint reversal (false victory or false defeat)
  - Consequences and tightening pressure
  - All Is Lost moment
  - Act 2 break: darkest hour, then revelation

- **Act 3 (Resolution)**: ~25% of total pages
  - New plan based on what was learned
  - Final confrontation / climax
  - Resolution of all major threads
  - Final image (mirrors or contrasts opening)

### 3. Sequence Breakdown
Map 8 sequences (each ~12-15 pages):
1. Setup and hook
2. Inciting incident and new direction
3. First obstacle and escalation
4. Midpoint shift
5. Consequences and complications
6. Lowest point
7. Climax build
8. Resolution

### 4. Character Arc Integration
- Protagonist's want vs. need mapped to structure
- Key relationship turning points placed at structural beats
- Antagonist pressure escalation aligned with act structure
- B-story woven through main structure

### 5. Pacing Assessment
- Action-to-dialogue ratio by act
- Scene length variation (short/punchy vs. longer dramatic scenes)
- Tension curve: does it build with appropriate valleys?
- Set pieces placed at structural high points

### 6. Structural Report
- Beat sheet with page numbers
- Sequence breakdown
- Structural strengths and weaknesses (analysis mode)
- Recommendations for structural improvements

## Output
- Complete beat sheet with page targets
- Sequence breakdown
- Structural analysis report (if analyzing existing work)
