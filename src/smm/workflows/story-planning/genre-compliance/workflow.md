# Genre Compliance Workflow

## Objective
Ensure story meets genre expectations while maintaining originality.

## Prerequisites
- Story structure completed
- Genre identified in SRD

## Steps

### 1. Identify Genre Requirements
Load genre-specific checklist:

**Romance:**
- Central love story
- Emotionally satisfying ending (HEA/HFN)
- Meet-cute or compelling first encounter
- Obstacles to relationship
- Character growth through love

**Mystery/Thriller:**
- Clear crime or threat
- Clues fairly planted
- Rising tension/stakes
- Satisfying resolution
- Protagonist actively investigates

**Fantasy:**
- Consistent magic system
- World-building depth
- Quest or conflict
- Good vs. evil elements
- Sense of wonder

**Science Fiction:**
- Speculative element central
- Internal logic consistent
- Explores "what if"
- Technology/science impacts plot
- Themes of humanity/progress

**Literary Fiction:**
- Character-driven
- Thematic depth
- Prose quality emphasis
- Ambiguity acceptable
- Internal journey focus

### 2. Check Required Elements
- List genre must-haves
- Verify each is present
- Note any missing elements

### 3. Evaluate Conventions
- Which conventions are honored?
- Which are subverted (intentionally)?
- Are subversions effective?

### 4. Reader Expectation Audit
- What does genre reader expect?
- Where might reader be disappointed?
- Where might reader be delighted?

### 5. Competitive Positioning
- How does story fit genre landscape?
- What makes it fresh?
- What makes it familiar enough?

### 6. Recommendations
- Elements to add
- Elements to strengthen
- Conventions to reconsider
- Unique angles to emphasize

### 7. Document Findings
- Genre compliance report
- Action items for revision

## Output
- Genre compliance report
- Recommendations for alignment
