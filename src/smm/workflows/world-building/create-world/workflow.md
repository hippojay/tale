# Create World Workflow

## Objective
Build comprehensive world foundation.

## Steps

### 1. Core Concept
- World type (real, alternate, fantasy, sci-fi)
- Time period
- Central premise/hook

### 2. Geography
- Map major regions
- Climate and terrain
- Resources and limitations

### 3. History
- Key historical events
- How history shapes present
- Conflicts and alliances

### 4. Society
- Government systems
- Social classes
- Power structures

### 5. Culture
- Beliefs and religions
- Customs and traditions
- Art and entertainment

### 6. Economy
- How people live
- Trade and commerce
- Wealth distribution

### 7. Rules
- Magic/technology systems
- Physical laws (if different)
- Limitations and costs

### 8. Daily Life
- What people eat, wear, do
- Transportation
- Communication

### 9. Document
- Add all to Story Bible
- Create world reference guide

## Output
- World foundation document
- Updated Story Bible
