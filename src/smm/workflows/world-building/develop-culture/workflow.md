# Develop Culture Workflow

## Objective
Create a detailed, internally consistent culture or society for the story world.

## Steps

### 1. Foundation
- What environment shaped this culture? (geography, climate, resources)
- How old is this civilization?
- What is the defining characteristic outsiders notice first?

### 2. Beliefs and Values
- Core belief system (religion, philosophy, or secular values)
- What does this culture consider sacred or taboo?
- How are moral judgments made?
- Relationship between belief and daily life

### 3. Social Structure
- How is power distributed? (monarchy, democracy, tribal, caste)
- Social classes and mobility between them
- Role of family and kinship
- Gender roles and expectations
- How are outsiders treated?

### 4. Daily Life
- What do people eat and how do they get food?
- How do people dress and why?
- What does a typical day look like?
- How do people travel and communicate?
- What do people do for entertainment?

### 5. Customs and Rituals
- Birth, coming-of-age, marriage, death customs
- Seasonal celebrations or festivals
- Greetings and social etiquette
- Conflict resolution traditions

### 6. Art and Expression
- Storytelling traditions
- Music, visual art, performance
- What is considered beautiful?
- How is history preserved?

### 7. Conflict and Tension
- Internal cultural tensions
- Relationship with neighboring cultures
- What threatens this culture's survival?
- How does this culture handle change?

### 8. Story Integration
- How does this culture create conflict for characters?
- What cultural rules will characters break or challenge?
- How does culture shape character worldview?

### 9. Document
- Add all details to Story Bible
- Note which details are shown vs. background knowledge

## Output
- Culture profile document
- Updated Story Bible
