# Expand Scene Workflow

## Objective
Expand thin or rushed scene with more detail and depth.

## Prerequisites
- Existing scene needing expansion
- Story Bible for consistency

## Steps

### 1. Diagnose Thinness
- Missing sensory detail?
- Rushed emotional beats?
- Insufficient conflict?
- Sparse dialogue?

### 2. Sensory Expansion
Add: visuals, sounds, smells, textures, tastes

### 3. Emotional Expansion
Deepen: reactions, internal responses, physical manifestations

### 4. Conflict Expansion
Strengthen: obstacles, tension, stakes

### 5. Pacing Check
- Don't over-expand
- Maintain momentum
- Balance additions

### 6. Continuity Verification
- Check Story Bible
- Update if new details added

## Output
- Expanded scene
- Story Bible updates if needed
