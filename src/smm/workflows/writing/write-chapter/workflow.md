# Write Chapter Workflow

## Objective
Transform chapter outline and story beats into fully realized prose with consistent voice, engaging narrative, and proper pacing.

## Prerequisites
- Chapter outline with scene beats and objectives
- Story Requirements Document (SRD)
- Story Bible (story-bible.md) for continuity
- Previous chapters for voice consistency (if applicable)

## Workflow Steps

### 1. Preparation
- Load and review the Story Requirements Document
- Load story-bible.md for character details, world information, and established facts
- Review chapter outline and understand scene objectives
- If not the first chapter, review previous 1-2 chapters for voice and continuity

### 2. Scene-by-Scene Writing
For each scene in the chapter outline:
- Establish clear POV character and setting
- Open with engaging hook or transition from previous scene
- Include sensory details appropriate to the setting
- Write natural dialogue that reveals character
- Show character emotions through action and reaction
- Advance plot according to outline beats
- Maintain appropriate pacing for scene type
- Close with transition or mini-cliffhanger

### 3. Chapter Polish
- Ensure opening hook is strong and engaging
- Verify pacing flows naturally throughout
- Check voice consistency with established style
- Confirm all outline beats are addressed
- Add chapter-ending hook for page-turning
- Verify continuity against story bible

### 4. Quality Check
- Read through for flow and rhythm
- Check for repetitive words or phrases
- Ensure sensory balance (not all visual)
- Verify dialogue tags are minimal and effective
- Confirm POV consistency throughout

### 5. Delivery
- Save chapter to appropriate location in output folder
- Flag any deviations from outline or continuity questions
- Note any new world details or character information for story bible
- Provide brief summary of chapter content

## Output Format
- Chapter file: `{output_folder}/chapters/chapter-{number}.md`
- Include chapter number and title at top
- Use markdown formatting for readability
- Include word count at end

## Notes for Agent
- Prioritize voice consistency over personal style
- When in doubt about details, check story bible first
- If outline is unclear, ask for clarification before proceeding
- Balance showing vs telling - dramatize key moments
- Keep reader engaged on every page
